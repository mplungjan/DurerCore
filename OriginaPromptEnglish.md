## SATURNIANA / DÜRER CORE

**Dürer Light v2 — Print Balanced**

## Main Tones

1. **Parchment Mist** — lightest background — `#D8CEBE`
2. **Antique Bone** — general light tone of the card — `#CDBEA9`
3. **Warm Stone** — architecture / secondary middle tone — `#B6A58F`
4. **Sepia Dust** — soft lines and medium engraving hatching — `#9A8874`
5. **Engraved Umber** — main shadows / clothing / depth — `#6F5E4F`
6. **Deep Sepia Ink** — darkest accents — `#3E342C`

## Palette Use

**Background:**  
`#D8CEBE`  
`#CDBEA9`

**Architecture / orbits / distant world:**  
`#B6A58F`  
`#9A8874`

**Figure / coat / main shadows:**  
`#6F5E4F`

**Deepest accents:**  
`#3E342C`

Background → Parchment Light / Antique Sand  
Engraving lines → Sepia Ink  
Deep shadows → Deep Engraving Brown / Near-Black Sepia  
Skin / face → Warm Stone + Antique Sand  
Clothing → Sepia Ink / Deep Engraving Brown  
Orbits → slightly lighter or thinner than the general drawing

The background is always light parchment, not dark.

The character is always slightly more real and dimensional than the background.

The engraving must remain airy. Orbits, hatching, and architecture must not overwhelm the figure.

# Title

**ARCHITECT OF FRACTURE**

All caps serif, tracking slightly open.

Optically: large, but not shouting.  
Approximately 7–9% of the card height.

For a card height of 127 mm, this gives approximately:

Visual letter height: ~8–11 mm  
Print terms: around 22–28 pt, then adjust optically.

Top title margin: approximately 8–10 mm from the upper edge of the safe zone.

Title block width: approximately 60–72% of the card width.

Classical serif, Roman capital, museum serif + engraved dignity.
